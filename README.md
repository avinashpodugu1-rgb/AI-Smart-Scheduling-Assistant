# AI Smart Scheduling Assistant

**Building AI course project**

## Summary
AI Smart Scheduling Assistant is a prototype AI-powered scheduling platform that helps businesses optimize appointments, workforce allocation, and resource reservations. It demonstrates how AI can reduce booking conflicts and improve customer experience.

## Background
Manual scheduling often causes double bookings, missed appointments, inefficient staff allocation, and revenue loss. This project proposes an AI assistant that recommends optimal schedules and predicts conflicts.

## How is it used?
Users submit booking requests, staff availability, and resource information. The AI recommends the best schedule, detects conflicts, and suggests alternatives.

## Data sources and AI methods
Sample booking and employee datasets are included.
Potential AI methods:
- Classification
- Recommendation systems
- Optimization
- Large Language Models
- Predictive analytics

## Challenges
The prototype does not include live integrations, payment systems, or real-time learning. Predictions depend on data quality.
